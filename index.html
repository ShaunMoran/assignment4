Stratocaster

A perfect choice for beginners, the Squier Strat Pack has everything you need to begin playing right out of the box.

The Stratocaster is ideal for beginning players and provides a comfortable playing feel. It includes a lightweight body, a vintage-style tremolo bridge for classic pitch effects and three single-coil pickups for classic Strat tone.

Ibanez RG120

The RG120 is an RG series solid body electric guitar model introduced by Ibanez in 2001. It is a special affordable spot model created for American musical instrument store chain Guitar Center. It was produced in both Korea and Indonesia through 2002, when Korean production was discontinued; after 2003 it was made in Indonesia exclusively.

Explorer

The Epiphone Explorer features the same futuristic design and angular styling that appeared on the famed 1958 Gibson classic. Its resonant mahogany solidbody construction gives it impressive sustain and a punchy midrange.

Fender CC-60SCE

Combining powerful onboard electronics—including a built-in tuner—with great tone and easy playability, the CC-60SCE is ideal for beginning to intermediate level players who are ready to plug in. The smaller concert-sized body is comfortable in any playing position, while the single-cutaway body provides easy access to the upper frets. Featuring a solid spruce top for increased volume and crisp sound, mahogany back and sides, and easy-to-play neck, the CC60-SCE is a perfect choice for the beach, the patio or the coffeehouse.

Squier P-Bass

A superb gateway into the time-honored Fender® family, the Squier® Affinity Series™ Precision Bass® PJ delivers legendary design and quintessential tone for today’s aspiring bassist. This P Bass® features several player-friendly refinements such as a slim and comfortable “C”-shaped neck profile, a 4-saddle bridge for solid string stability, and vintage-style open-gear tuning machines for smooth, accurate tuning. Loaded with a Squier split-coil P Bass neck pickup and a single-coil J Bass® bridge pickup for a wide variety of tones, this model is ready to help lay the foundation for any player at any stage.
